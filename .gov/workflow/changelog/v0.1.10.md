# StratAtlas v0.1.10

Date: 2026-03-09
Status: Planned release entry for `WP-GOV-INSTALLER-003`

## Highlights

- Governed release promotion now archives prior current releases and migrates legacy `.product/build_target/Current` installer history into versioned archive storage.
- The current installer surface, portable executable surface, and timestamped installer kit are all produced together with lifecycle guidance and the matching changelog copy.
- Installer and executable outputs remain gitignored across release-current, release-archive, and old-version folders.
- The maintenance menu continues to expose install, repair, uninstall, full uninstall, update, downgrade, and changelog/help paths in operator-facing language.

## Operator Notes

- Current release assets remain isolated under `.product/build_target/Releases/Current/`.
- Superseded release assets and migrated legacy installer history remain isolated under `.product/build_target/Releases/Archive/<version>/`.
- Full uninstall and full clean reinstall remain explicit opt-in maintenance paths; the default maintenance flows preserve user data and presets.
